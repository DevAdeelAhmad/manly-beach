Welcome to Manly Beach

Gateway to the Northern Beaches of Sydney

Location of Manly

Gateway to the Northern Beaches

Manly is the first beach-side suburb you encounter in northern Sydney coming from Sydney CBD.

Vibrant Destination

A must-visit place frequented by a youthful and international population, famous for being the first Australian beach to hold a world surfing contest.

Natural Gifts

Enjoy tranquil beaches, rich marine reserves, National Parks, lagoons, and hidden natural asset gems.

Cultural History and Heritage

Indigenous Roots

The Cannalgal and Kayaimai communities originally inhabited Manly.

Colonial Era

In 1788, Arthur Philip named the area "Manly" due to the "confidence and manly behavior" of the Aboriginal people.

Modern Development

From a Victorian village to a vibrant suburb, Manly has evolved into a diverse community dependent on tourism.
